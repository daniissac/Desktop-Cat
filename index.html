Kitty in Desk: Your Purr-fect Desktop Companion!

Meet Your New Digital Pet!

Kitty in Desk brings a cute, animated cat right to your desktop. Watch as it sleeps, wanders, and keeps you company while you work!

Features:

  • Adorable animations: idle, sleep, and walk
  • Stays on top of other windows
  • Wanders around your screen
  • Collision detection to stay within screen bounds
  • Smooth, modern UI with PyQt6
  • Keeps your screen awake
